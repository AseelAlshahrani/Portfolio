Supernova

Explore space, discover stars, and learn while playing!

During my studies, I had the opportunity to collaborate with a team to develop an educational video game as part of our graphic design project.

The game, titled "Supernova", follows the journey of an astronaut attempting to reach the moon. As players navigate through the game, they encounter various challenges that they must overcome to progress towards their goal. "Supernova" aims to educate players about space exploration and the perseverance required to achieve ambitious goals. The game was designed with captivating visuals and engaging gameplay mechanics to ensure that players not only enjoy the experience but also learn from it. "Supernova" represents our dedication to creativity, innovation, and the use of technology for educational purposes.
