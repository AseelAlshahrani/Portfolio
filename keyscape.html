Keyscape Game Objects

About The Game

In this 2D adventure game, you control Leesa, a character navigating through various levels. The objective is to avoid enemies, collect keys, and unlock doors to progress through the game. Each level presents new challenges and obstacles, making it a thrilling journey filled with strategy and quick reflexes.

Lisa Character

Your main playable character. She's fast, smart, and must navigate through challenges to collect the key and reach the door.

Key

The objective of each level. Lisa must collect this key to unlock the door and win the level.

Door

Found at the end of each level. Once the key is collected, the door opens to allow Lisa to escape.

Enemy Bird

Flies across the scene to try and stop Lisa. Colliding with this enemy resets the game.
